Bias and Misinformation in Health Care Experiences with Black Americans

We want to hear from you!

  • About the Project

We are researchers at the Equity and Health Innovations (EHI) Design Research Lab at Carnegie Mellon University looking to recruit adults who identify as Black or African American to participate in a design interview study. 

The goal of these design interview sessions is to understand how Black people navigate bias and misinformation as it relates to healthcare. In particular, we want to understand how we can design a chatbot to help Black people understand and navigate healthcare misinformation they may encounter in their lives.
